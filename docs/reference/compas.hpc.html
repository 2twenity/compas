cuda

cuda_diag(a) Construct or extract GPUArray diagonal.
cuda_eye(n[, bit]) Create GPUArray identity matrix (ones on diagonal) of size (n x n).
cuda_get(a) Get back GPUArray from GPU memory as NumPy array.
cuda_give(a[, bit, type]) Give a list or an array to GPU memory.
cuda_ones(shape[, bit]) Create GPUArray of ones directly on GPU memory.
cuda_random(shape[, bit]) Create random values in the range [0, 1] as GPUArray.
cuda_real(a) Return the real parts of GPUArray.
cuda_reshape(a, shape) Reshape a GPUArray.
cuda_flatten(a) Flatten/ravel out a GPUArray.
cuda_tile(a, shape) Horizontally and vertically tile a GPUArray.
cuda_zeros(shape[, bit]) Create GPUArray of zeros directly on GPU memory.
cuda_conj(a) Complex conjugate of GPUArray elements.
cuda_cross(a, b, bsize) Cross-product of two GPUArrays (row by row).
cuda_det(a) GPUArray square matrix determinant.
cuda_dot(a, b) Matrix multiplication of two GPUArrays.
cuda_eig(a) Matrix Eigenvectors and Eigenvalues of GPUArray.
cuda_hermitian(a) Hermitian conjugate transpose of GPUArray.
cuda_inv(a) Inverse of GPUArray matrix.
cuda_normrow(a) GPUArray of vectors norm.2 (row by row).
cuda_pinv(a) Moore-Penrose pseudo inverse of the GPUArray.
cuda_svd(a[, jobu, jobvt]) GPUArray Singular Value Decomposition.
cuda_trace(a) GPUArray trace, the sum along the main diagonal.
cuda_transpose(a) Transpose a GPUArray.
cuda_abs(a) Absolute values of GPUArray elements.
cuda_argmax(a, axis) Location of maximum GPUArray elements.
cuda_argmin(a, axis) Location of minimum GPUArray elements.
cuda_acos(a) Trigonometric arccosine of GPUArray elements.
cuda_asin(a) Trigonometric arcsine of GPUArray elements.
cuda_atan(a) Trigonometric arctangent of GPUArray elements.
cuda_ceil(a) Ceiling of GPUArray elements.
cuda_cos(a) Trigonometric cosine of GPUArray elements.
cuda_cosh(a) Hyperbolic cosine of GPUArray elements.
cuda_exp(a) Exponential of GPUArray elements.
cuda_floor(a) Floor of GPUArray elements.
cuda_log(a) Natural logarithm of GPUArray elements.
cuda_max(a, axis) Maximum values of GPUArray elements.
cuda_min(a, axis) Minimum values of GPUArray elements.
cuda_mean(a, axis) Mean of GPUArray elements in a given axis direction.
cuda_sin(a) Trigonometric sine of GPUArray elements.
cuda_sinh(a) Hyperbolic sine of GPUArray elements.
cuda_sqrt(a) Square-root of GPUArray elements.
cuda_sum(a, axis) Sum of GPUArray elements in a given axis direction.
cuda_tan(a) Trigonometric tangent of GPUArray elements.
cuda_tanh(a) Hyperbolic tangent of GPUArray elements.
cuda_device() Displays the CUDA GPU device details.
